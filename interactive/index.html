FOOD

Here is a list of food that you cannot miss to try while you are visiting. These are traditional recipies passed down from generation to generation. They are a culinary treasure, that you cannot find anywhere else.

Chicatanas

Chicatanas, an insect that gives you more flavor and folklore to the ancient and traditional cuisine of Oaxaca, something different to try, another particular flavor of Oaxaca.

Tlayuda

“Mexican pizza” but really more of a flatbread, a tlayuda starts with a very large, thin, crispy corn tortilla that is baked on a comal or wood grill.

Tamales

Oaxacan style tamales, a traditional Mesoamerican dish made of masa or dough which is steamed in a corn husk or banana leaf.

Chapulines

The chapulines (grasshoppers) are one of the most important, edible insects in Mexico and have been a vital part of local diets for centuries.

TRIP ESSENTIALS

You must pack:

Sweatters/Jackets
Sandles
